Quantum Py

Libraries Used : tkinter, numpy, qiskit :: Visualizes effect of different Quantum Gates for single Qubit Rotation on Bloch Sphere. Bloch Sphere(definition) : In quantum mechanics and computing, the Bloch sphere is a geometrical representation of the pure state space of a two-level quantum mechanical system (qubit), named after the physicist Felix Bloch. The code uses IBM's Qiskit, an open-source SDK for working with quantum computers at the level of pulses, circuits, and application modules.

A Simple Approach for Extending Up to the Ultrarelativistic Limit the Theory of a Non-Relativistic Fermi Gas

A unified description of a Fermi gas at low temperatures is presented. This description is valid from the non-relativistic to the ultra-relativistic limits. Moreover, it is simple. The presented theory is based on a recently reported formula for the energies of a relativistic spin-0 particle in a box. The theory follows the relatively simple approach often used for constructing the theory of a non-relativistic Fermi gas. Click below to read the full paper.
